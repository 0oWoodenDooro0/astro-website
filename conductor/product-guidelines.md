# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Aesthetic:** Developer/Hacker Aesthetic. The design should feel like a comfortable coding environment.
- **Color Palette:** **Catppuccin Mocha Palette**.
  - Backgrounds: Dark, muted tones (Base/Mantle/Crust).
  - **Primary Accent:** **Pink** (Catppuccin Pink).
  - Secondary Accents: Other Catppuccin colors (Mauve, Blue, Lavender) used sparingly for support.
  - Text: High readability without harsh contrast (Text/Subtext).
- **Typography:**
  - **Font Family:** **JetBrains Mono** for *all* elements (headers, body, navigation).
  - **Vibe:** Technical, precise, and reminiscent of a terminal or IDE.

## Tone of Voice
- **Style:** Technical & Concise.
- **Approach:** Direct and to the point. Avoid fluff. Focus on clarity, accuracy, and providing value efficiently, similar to high-quality technical documentation.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Efficiency:** Navigation should be fast and logical.
- **Readability:** High contrast and clear spacing to make code blocks and technical content easy to scan.
- **Authenticity:** The site should feel like a tool built by a developer, for developers (and tech-savvy visitors).
